My Story

The Story Behind Jewels by J

Jewels by J was founded in 2024 by Jerrilyn Mensah, a university student who turned her passion for jewelry into a growing creative journey. As a sole proprietorship, every piece is designed with care, blending elegance, simplicity, and meaning for everyday wear.

I believe jewelry is not just an accessory, but a wearable memoir. From initial sketch to the final polish, each item receives individual artistry to ensure its brilliance lasts for generations. Every piece is thoughtfully brought to life with attention to detail, capturing moments, emotions, and personal stories in designs meant to be worn and cherished every day.
